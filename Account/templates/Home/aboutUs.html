WELCOME TO APPLE NAILS & SPA

Apple Nails and Spa is proud and honored to be voted top 10 by our community with The Best of Omaha for 10 years in a row for manicures & pedicures. We know how important it is to have highly trained experienced professionals that are licensed nail technicians. We also have a dedicated staff that is friendly, knowledgeable and committed to providing an an exceptional customer service experience.

We have a very clean & sanitized environment, state of the art ventilation system and offer a wide variety of services using high quality products for a superior performance.

We have recently installed splash guards, Brand NEW pedicure chairs, UV sanitizing lights, and an air purification system.

Please call 402-991-7887 to set up an appointment. All of us at Apple Nails & Spa are so excited to see you and make you feel pampered.
